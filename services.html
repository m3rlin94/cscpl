Services

Each and every Lion member of our club is trying to change lives in ways both great and small. Service is a personal journey that impacts the people serving and the people we serve. These are some of our story, right from the frontlines of service.

Main Service Areas

Following are the service areas we are mostly focussing on

Diabetes

We serve to reduce the prevalence of diabetes and improve quality of life for those diagnosed.

Vision

We serve to prevent avoidable blindness and improve quality of life for people who are blind and visually impaired.

Environment

We serve to sustainably protect and restore our environment to improve the well-being of all communities.

Childhood Cancer

We serve to help those affected by childhood cancer survive and thrive.

Hunger

We serve to ensure all community members have access to nutritious foods.

Service Projects of Our Club

This is how we contributed to our society during the Lionistic Year of 2018/2019




Hunger Relief

15th September, 2018

Lions Club of Colombo Second Century Pioneer Leo along with Lions Club of Gampaha Metro and Leo Club of Gampaha Metro successfully held the soup donation project for patients of Gampaha Base Hospital on 15th September, 2018.

සිනහ යාත්‍රා

24th September, 2018

We have successfully carried out "නිදිගත් හීන ඇහැරන්නට සිනහ යාත්‍රා" project for 45 girls in Eheliyagoda Paleegala Sarath Ukwaththage Childrens' Home in Rathnapura District on 24th of September, 2018

Cataract Operation

30th October, 2018

We sponsored for a cataract operation under the project category, "Vision". This project was a initial step of a continouous project our club is carrying out monthly by donating the admission fee for the needy people and direct them to Kadawatha Golden Jubilee Eye Hospital.

හැන්දෑව

24th November, 2018

Handewa (හැන්දෑව) was one of the biggest projects carried out by the club where we were able to serve elders in Aggy Jayasekara Elders' Home in Gampaha under all five (5) of our main service areas which are Diabetes, Hunger, Vision, Environment, and Health.

Hunger Relief

24th December, 2018

Senehase Naththal (සෙනෙහසේ නත්තල්) project was carried out by the Lions Club of Colombo Second Century Pioneer Leo where we able to successfully distribute goods and food items worth of Rs. 26,480 for five (5) needy families around Kelaniya area, under the Hunger Relief project category.

Use Plants to Bring Life

25th January, 2019

Lions Club of Colombo Second Century Pioneer Leo was able to successfully distribute 85 mango plants to schools and temples within Rathnapura district on 25th January, 2019.
