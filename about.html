About Us

Get to know about our club and the amazing team behind everything.

Extension Meeting - 21st July 2017
Extended By - Lion Shyama Silva MJF (DG 2017/2018)
First Monthly Meeting - 19th August 2017

Lions Club of Colombo Second Century Pioneer Leo was extended at the Centennial Lions Convention which was held in Chicago. It was extended by the Lions Club of Ragama Welisara under the patronage of Lion Shyama Silva MJF, District Governor 2017/2018, and with the guidance of Lion Udaya Silva MJF JP and Lion Rajitha Abeygunasekara, Leo Club Program Advisory Panelist. The club was chartered with 20 members, Leo Lion Nimesh Samarasekara being the first club president for fiscal year 2017/2018. Second fiscal year 2018/2019 started with a growth of membership and a well organized series of service projects, Leo Lion Prabuddhika Wijethunge presiding the club.

The club is currently operating with 35+ active members, organizing more than 10 service projects per year, covering global causes of Lions Clubs International along with unique initiatives. The successful third year 2019/2020 started with the installation ceremony of the new committee held on 23rd of June 2019, which appointed Leo Lion Naveen C. Cooray as the new president.
